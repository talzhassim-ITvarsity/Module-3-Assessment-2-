DESERTS

MOLTEN LAVA CAKE

A classic dessert that features a rich and decadent chocolate cake with a gooey, molten chocolate center. It's often served warm and paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Crème Brûlée

A silky and creamy custard dessert with a caramelized sugar crust on top. It has a delicate vanilla flavor and a satisfying contrast between the smooth custard and the crackling caramelized layer.

TIRAMISU

An Italian classic dessert consisting of layers of ladyfingers soaked in coffee and liqueur, layered with a rich and creamy mascarpone cheese filling, and dusted with cocoa powder. It's a sophisticated and indulgent dessert.
